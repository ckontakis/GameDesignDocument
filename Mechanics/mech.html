Explain Mechanics



Rule Addition




Win : The player has to defeat the enemy to win the game

Loss : The player has to lose all their lives, to lose the game


























Controls Addition




W : Move forward

A : Move to the left

S : Move backwards

D : Move to the right

Mouse : The player can move the camera with their mouse

Left click : Push buttons and interact with objects
